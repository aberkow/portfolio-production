I'm Adam, a web developer based in West Hartford, CT.

About Me

This year I have begun my second career as a web developer. My family and I live in West Hartford CT, where I have been a professional clarinetist and teacher for the last eight years. I believe that my skills as a musician (attention to detail, willingness to experiment, devotion to practicing a craft) will help me succeed on this new path. When I'm not coding or practicing, my family is the major influence on my life. My wife and I have two sons one of whom has Fragile X Syndrome and the other is a carrier. My goal is to combine these aspects of my life through technology and coding.
