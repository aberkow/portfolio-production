About Me

This year I have begun my second career as a web developer. To do that, I enrolled in Thinkful's front-end developer career track. They offer a practical, mentor led approach to learning to code which has been an excellent experience for me. For instance, this portfolio is one of the projects I've made recently. I decided to create it using Bootstrap to see how far I could push the skills I've learned so far.

My family and I live in West Hartford CT, where I have been a professional clarinetist and teacher at the University of Hartford's Hartt Community Division for the last eight years. I believe that my skills as a musician (attention to detail, willingness to experiment, devotion to practicing a craft) will help me succeed on this new path. When I'm not coding or practicing, my family is the major influence on my life. My wife and I have two sons one of whom has Fragile X Syndrome and the other is a carrier.

My current goals as a developer are to learn as much as I can both about music and the web as well as accessibility for people with special needs. It is my hope that this way I can combine these important aspects of my life with technology and coding.

Karma Landing Page

This project demonstrates how I was able to expand my skills in order to make a copy of Karma's old landing page. I think my version is extremely close to the original. This challenged me to think about how a webpage is first wireframed and divided into different sections. From there, I worked on each section one at a time first organizing the HTML then applying the CSS. This project also helped me develop a better understanding of Chrome's dev tools and Git/Github. By the end of this project I understood much more about how websites can be organized, tested, and built.

Languages and skills: HTML, CSS, Chrome Dev Tools, Git/Github
